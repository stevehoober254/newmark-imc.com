Our company

The Newmark Group Limited is a leading African Integrated Marketing Communications (IMC) firm with a strong presence in the Multinational Brand segment of the market. With a footprint in over 30 countries in Africa, Newmark brings to its’ clients a multicultural and authentically localized approach to messaging, strategy and engagement.
